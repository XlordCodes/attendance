## 🎨 **Design Principles Applied**

### **1. Visual Hierarchy**
- **Font Weights**: 
  - Black (900): Main headers, company name
  - Bold (700): Section headers, labels
  - Semibold (600): Navigation items
  - Medium (500): Body text, descriptions

### **2. Contrast & Accessibility**
- **High Contrast**: Pure black on white for maximum readability
- **Clear Borders**: Black borders for component separation
- **Hover States**: Subtle gray transitions

### **3. Consistency**
- **No Rounded Corners**: Sharp, geometric design
- **Uniform Spacing**: Consistent padding and margins
- **Monochrome Icons**: All Lucide React icons in black/white
